This is Xiao He. Welcome to my homepage!

I am currently a third-year PhD student under the supervision of Prof. Mehdi Vahdati in Department of Mechanical Engineering, Imperial College London. Before joining Imperial, I was with the Turbo Lab in Tsinghua University where I received a BEng (Hons) in Automotive Engineering and an MSc in Power Engineering and Engineering Thermophysics.

My research interest in general includes the field of turbulence, data science and their applications to turbomachinery. My PhD research focuses on data-driven turbulence modeling for compressor stall. Please find the details in the Research page.
